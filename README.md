# Pharmaceutical Market Intelligence & Growth Analytics
##Analysis of Oncology Drug Sales in Egypt (2022–2025)
Comparing Local vs. Imported Oncology Medicines in Egypt

---

## Project Overview
This report analyzes the Egyptian pharmaceutical market with a focus on oncology drugs.  
This project aims to understand market structure, pricing differences, and the role of local vs. imported medicines.

---

## Problem Statement
The oncology market in Egypt shows strong reliance on imported medicines, particularly in high-value treatments.

This analysis aims to answer:
- Market size and growth
- Differences between local and imported products
- Key drivers of market value

---

## Objectives
- Measure oncology sales (2022–2025)
- Compare local vs. imported products in terms of value and units
- Identify top products, corporations, and categories
- Analyze pricing differences
- Highlight market concentration

---

## Data Description

### Source Columns
Sector, ATC4, Corporation, Product, Pack, Launch Date, Strength, Retail Price, NFC3, Period, Calendar Year, Units, LC Value.

### Cleaned / Final Dataset Fields
Sector, Corporation, Product, Category, Units, Revenue (EGP), Unit Price, Time (Period / Year), Local vs Imported.

## Dashboard Summary
The Power BI dashboard includes:

### 1. Executive Overview
Provides the main summary KPIs and market trends.

### 2. Executive Overview 2

### 3. Cancer Market Overview
Focuses on cancer-specific metrics such as revenue, units, average price, and market share.

### 4. Local vs Imported Cancer Comparison
Compares market performance between local and imported products.

### 5. Companies and Competition
Analyzes top companies and competition levels.

### 6. Opportunity & Recommendation Page
Highlights high-gap products, imported dependence, and local presence.

---

## Key Insights
- The oncology market is highly concentrated in a limited number of products and companies.
- Imported medicines dominate market value (approximately 91.5%).
- A significant price gap exists.
- Local products compete more on volume than value.

---

## Recommendations
- Focus localization on high-value imported products.
- Target categories with high import dependency.
- Monitor pricing to improve affordability.
- Use dashboards for continuous market tracking.
